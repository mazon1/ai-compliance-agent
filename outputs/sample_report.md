# Sample Compliance Report

**Document:** non_compliant.txt  
**Overall status:** Non-Compliant

## Summary
The procedure is missing several key requirements expected in a defensible fire extinguisher inspection process. It uses vague wording where specific frequencies and checks are required.

## Findings
1. **Missing monthly inspection requirement**  
   - Severity: High  
   - Recommendation: Specify that extinguishers must be visually inspected at least monthly.

2. **Annual maintenance is vague**  
   - Severity: High  
   - Recommendation: Replace “from time to time” with a specific annual maintenance requirement.

3. **Accessibility expectations are unclear**  
   - Severity: Medium  
   - Recommendation: State that extinguishers must be visible, accessible, and not obstructed.

4. **Condition checks are incomplete**  
   - Severity: Medium  
   - Recommendation: Add specific checks for damage, corrosion, pressure gauge status, and tamper seal.

## Strengths
- The document recognizes that extinguishers should be checked.
- The document references damaged extinguishers, although the response process is vague.
